Your First Visit

What is Bouldering?

Our team of dedicated and certified instructors is here to support your climbing journey. Get to know the friendly faces behind Boulderpalooza who are always ready to assist, encourage, and ensure your climbing experience is unforgettable.

What to Expect

At Boulderpalooza, we cater to climbers of all skill levels, including beginners. Here's what you can expect when you come in for your first bouldering session:

  • Safety First: Our staff will provide you with a brief orientation on safety procedures and proper use of climbing equipment.
  • Bouldering Shoes: We offer rental climbing shoes designed for better grip and support while climbing.
  • Chalk: Chalk is available to keep your hands dry and improve your grip on the holds.
  • Boulder Routes: You'll find routes of varying difficulty levels, so you can choose one that matches your abilities.
  • Friendly Atmosphere: Our community is welcoming and inclusive, so don't hesitate to ask for help or tips.

Your First Climb

When you're ready to take your first climb, follow these steps:

  • Select a Beginner's Route: Look for routes marked with beginner-friendly holds and follow the color-coded tape.
  • Warm-Up: Spend a few minutes stretching and warming up your muscles.
  • Start Climbing: Begin your climb, focusing on balance, technique, and controlled movements.
  • Ask for Help: If you encounter any difficulties or have questions, our staff and fellow climbers are here to assist you.

Climbing Etiquette

  • Respect others: Wait your turn and be mindful of fellow climbers.
  • Keep the gym clean: Dispose of chalk and trash properly.
  • Safety first: Always follow safety guidelines and listen to staff instructions.
